How can we help you??

How do I sign in to Task Manager?

Depending on your task assignment, you can sign in to Task Manager with Organisation Account


Ready to sign in?

You must have an active internet connection to sign in. If you already know how to sign in to Task Manager, go to taskmanager.com


Get started with Task Manager

To get started, complete these steps:

  • Sign in to Task Manager.
  • You can assign task by clicking on add task.
  • View task through assigned tasks.
  • View task assigned by you->By clicking assigned task.
  • View the progress of the project through dashboard
